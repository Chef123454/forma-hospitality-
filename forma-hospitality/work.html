The Shore House, Krabi

The Shore House had been operating for 6 years with a WordPress site that was slow, mobile-unfriendly, and visually mismatched with the property's actual quality. The owners were losing direct bookings to OTAs because guests couldn't see what made the hotel special.

We audited the site, redesigned from scratch with new photography direction, integrated Cloudbeds for direct booking, and launched a full 6-page website within 12 days.
