FAQ

Pricing
questions answered.

Still unsure? Just ask. We reply within 24 hours and are always happy to talk through your project before you commit to anything.

Ask a question →
Do prices include hosting? +
Our prices cover the design and build. We recommend and help you set up hosting on Netlify (free tier covers most sites). Domain registration is separate — typically $15–20/year. We'll walk you through everything.
What do you need from me to start? +
A short discovery call, any existing brand assets (logo, photos, colours if you have them), and answers to a brief intake form. If you don't have photos yet, we can advise on what to shoot and how — or work with placeholder content initially.
Can I pay in instalments? +
Yes. Standard terms are 50% to begin and 50% on delivery. For Premium projects we can discuss a three-stage payment schedule. All payments via bank transfer, Wise, or Stripe.
What if I need more pages than the package includes? +
Additional pages can be added to any package at $197 per page. We'll always flag this upfront if we think you need more than the package covers — no surprises.
Do you offer ongoing support after the 30 days? +
Yes — we offer optional monthly maintenance plans starting from $197/month. This covers content updates, security patches, performance monitoring, and general upkeep. Not required, but available if you'd prefer not to handle it yourself.
What currency do you accept? +
USD, GBP, EUR, and THB. We'll invoice in your preferred currency. Payments accepted via bank transfer, Wise, or Stripe.
